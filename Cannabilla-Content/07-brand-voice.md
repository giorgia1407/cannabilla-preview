# Cannabilla — Brand Voice & Language Reference

Source: live product descriptions, Ecwid `26630034`, 2026-07-10 · READ-ONLY.

## 1. Tone in one line
Warm, expert, botanical, unmistakably Italian — a cosmetics-lab voice rooted in the Monti Sibillini. Benefit-first, ingredient-led, compliance-aware (leads with **canapa**, keeps clinical/CBD detail lower in the body).

## 3. Recurring structural blocks (every cosmetic follows this skeleton)
1. **Intro** — benefit + hero ingredient (olio di canapa), skin types addressed.
2. `🧴 Formato:` size · `♻` packaging note · `🌿` natural-variation disclaimer.
3. **`Come usarlo:`** — application ritual, often cross-sells another Cannabilla product.
4. Storage/safety: *"Solo per uso esterno…"*
5. **`Cosa contiene:`** — `🍃 Ingrediente: beneficio` bulleted list.
6. CPNP compliance line + **`INCI:`** full list.
